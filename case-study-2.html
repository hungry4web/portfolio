LOGO & BRANDING DESIGN - CASE STUDY

I was asked to design a logo and develop branding for a new online social network platform aimed at being a fun and upbeat place for people to gather online to discuss everyday topics and help each other cope by adding a couple laughs to their day. Since the site revolves around online discussions I used talk bubbles for the logo, and chose bright playful colors.

The next step was designing a homepage, and I used responsive web design to build this so that it looks fabulous across all devices. The text is simple, to entice, and the green button large and bold, encouraing the next step to jump into the site and check it all out. The background image is subtle, a playful image of a child blowing bubbles, and then profile photos also acting as transparent bubbles. All playing off the inspiration from the talk bubble and the platform of social media and online connections.
